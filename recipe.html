Avocado Toast

Ingredients: Bread, avocado, lemon, salt, pepper.

Instructions: Toast the bread, mash avocado with lemon juice, season, and spread generously.

Pancakes

Ingredients: Flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, milk, egg, melted butter, vanilla extract.

Instructions: In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, butter, and vanilla. Pour wet into dry ingredients. Mix until just combined. Heat a greased pan on medium. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form, flip and cook other side until golden brown.

Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients: Eggs, milk, salt, pepper, butter.

Instructions: Crack eggs into a bowl, add milk, salt, and pepper. Whisk until smooth. Melt butter in a nonstick pan over medium-low heat. Pour in the eggs and stir gently with a spatula until soft curds form. Remove from heat when still slightly runny—they'll finish cooking off the heat.

Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients: Spaghetti, eggs, bacon, parmesan, black pepper.

Instructions: Cook pasta, mix hot with eggs and cheese, stir in bacon, season with pepper.

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio with Prawns

Ingredients: spaghetti, prawns, garlic, chili flakes, olive oil, salt& black pepper, fresh parsley, grated Parmesan, lemon wedge.

Instructions: Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water before draining. In a pan, heat olive oil on medium heat. Add garlic and cook until just golden (don’t burn). Add chili flakes and stir quickly. Add the prawns, cook until they turn pink (2–3 mins). Add cooked spaghetti into the pan, toss everything together. Add pasta water gradually to loosen the sauce. Season with salt and black pepper. Top with parsley and Parmesan. Serve with lemon wedge if desired.

Instructions: In a large bowl, toss lettuce with Caesar dressing. Add croutons and Parmesan. Toss again just before serving.

Beef Stir Fry

Ingredients: Beef strips, soy sauce, oyster sauce, cornstarch, garlic, broccoli, bell peppers, carrots, oil.

Instructions: Marinate beef in soy sauce, oyster sauce, and cornstarch (15 mins). Heat oil in wok, cook garlic and beef until browned. Add vegetables, stir-fry for 3–4 mins. Add a splash of water if needed. Cook until veg are tender-crisp.

Salmon Teriyaki

Ingredients: Salmon fillets, soy sauce, honey or brown sugar, mirin or rice vinegar, grated ginger, sesame oil.

Instructions: Mix soy sauce, mirin, sake, ginger, and garlic to make the teriyaki sauce. Marinate salmon for 15–30 minutes in half the sauce. Heat a non-stick pan and cook salmon skin-side down first. Flip and cook other side, then pour in remaining sauce. Simmer until the sauce thickens and coats the salmon. Serve with rice and vegetables.

Creamy Mushroom Pasta

Ingredients: pasta(fettucine or spaghetti), olive oil, garlic, onio, mushrooms, cooking cream or heavy cream, grated Parmesan Cheese, salt& black pepper.

Instructions: Cook the pasta in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve a little pasta water, then drain. In a pan, heat olive oil/butter over medium heat. Sauté onions and garlic until soft. Add the sliced mushrooms. Cook until browned and soft (about 5–7 minutes). Pour in the cream, stir well, and let it simmer for 2–3 minutes. Stir in Parmesan cheese and mix until melted and creamy. Season with salt, pepper, and optional nutmeg. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat. If needed, add a bit of pasta water to loosen the sauce. Garnish with chopped parsley and more cheese if desired.

Banana Muffins

Ingredients: Banana, melted butter, sugar, egg, vanilla extract, baking soda, salt, all-purpose flour.

Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F).

2. Line muffin tin. In a bowl, mix mashed bananas and butter.

3. Stir in sugar, egg, and vanilla. Add baking soda, salt, and flour.

4. Mix just until combined. Pour into muffin cups and bake 20–25 minutes.

5. Cool before serving.

Tiramisu Cake

Ingredients: ladyfinger biscuits, strong brewed espresso, cocoa powder, mascarpone cheese, whipping cream, powdered sugar, vanilla extract.

Instructions:

1. Prepare the Coffee Dip: In a shallow bowl, mix cooled coffee with liqueur (if using). Set aside for dipping the ladyfingers.

2. Make the Cream Filling: In a large bowl, beat mascarpone with vanilla and powdered sugar until smooth. In another bowl, whip the cream until stiff peaks form. Gently fold whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture until fully combined and fluffy.

3. Assemble the Tiramisu: Quickly dip each ladyfinger into the coffee mixture (1–2 seconds per side, don’t soak too long). Layer the dipped ladyfingers in a dish (about 8x8 inch or similar). Spread half of the cream filling evenly over the ladyfingers. Repeat another layer of dipped ladyfingers. Spread remaining cream on top and smooth it out.

4. Chill & Serve: Cover and refrigerate for at least 6 hours (overnight is best for flavor and texture). Before serving, dust the top generously with unsweetened cocoa powder. Optional: garnish with grated chocolate or chocolate curls.
